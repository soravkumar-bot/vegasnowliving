Living Options

Apartment Types

Whatever your budget or lifestyle, there's a space designed for you.

Compact Living

Studio Apartments

Smart, efficient, and easy to maintain. Ideal for solo dwellers who value location over square footage.

Classic Choice

1-Bedroom Apartments

The sweet spot — a dedicated bedroom, open living area, and room to breathe without breaking the budget.

Urban Style

Loft Spaces

High ceilings, open plans, and industrial character. Perfect for creatives and those who love distinctive design.

Community

Co-Living Spaces

Furnished rooms in shared homes with communal kitchens and lounges. Great for newcomers to the city.

Space & Comfort

2-Bedroom Apartments

Room for a home office, a roommate, or a growing family. More space, more possibility.

Premium

Penthouse & Luxury

Panoramic views, premium finishes, and concierge access. For those who want the very best city living offers.

Renting Tips for Urban Living

Make your next lease the best one yet.

Budget

The 30% Rule

Keep rent under 30% of your monthly take-home. Leave room for food, savings, and city experiences.

Lease

Read Every Clause

Pet policies, subletting rules, and early-exit fees. Know what you're agreeing to before you sign anything.
