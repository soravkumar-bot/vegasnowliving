Tips

Before You Choose a Neighborhood

A few smart things to check before signing a lease.

Commute

Test Your Daily Commute

Visit at rush hour. Walk the route. Check transit lines and parking. A 10-minute drive can become 40 minutes in traffic.

Walkability

Count What's Within 10 Minutes

Grocery store, café, gym, pharmacy. The more you can walk to, the less your daily stress.
